Who I am

I'm from Colombia (South America), mixed from the afrocaribbean swing, arabic ancestry, and the kindness of the Hispanic/native breeding that enriched the universality of my region.

I grew up humility as an average kid between Bogota (Colombia's capital), the Caribbean coast and islands.

I always liked languages, science, math and history, but also, I was good for sports, specially in Basketball.

I had my first girlfriend from the States when I was 14, that helped me to speak better English, before living and sharing with Jamaicans and San Andres Islanders later.

Always wanted to become part of the military forces, after trial and error, and painful experiences in different branches of the Navy and the Army, I became an officer in the reserve Corp.

During those years in and out from the Navy processes, I achieved the seamen training to work overseas, a certificate in Krav maga and learned Arabic Fosha (able to read and write in aliphatic), lastly, I wanted to learn Russian in the same way (able to read Cyrillic), but I had to suspend to start my studies in Canada.

I feel like I made many dreams that I had as a boy came true, not from one day to another, but with patience and wisdom, and during the journey, I see the value on the service to others, and to myself.

Now I have a family life, sharing experiences together and projecting a future as a family in Canada.
